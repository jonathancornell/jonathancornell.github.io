Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 24, 2026 | Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Jonathan Cornell ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit jonathancornell.com (the "Site") or engage my consulting services.

Information I Collect

Information You Provide

When you schedule a consultation or contact me, I collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company name and role (if provided)
  • Consultation details and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device information

Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • zcal.co - Scheduling and calendar management
  • Stripe - Payment processing (for paid consultations)
  • Google Analytics (if implemented) - Website analytics

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

How I Use Your Information

I use your information to:

  • Schedule and conduct consultations
  • Process payments for consulting services
  • Send confirmation emails and calendar invites
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send follow-up materials and action plans
  • Improve my services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

I may share your information with:

  • Service providers (zcal, Stripe) to deliver services
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Professional advisors (lawyers, accountants) under confidentiality agreements

Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure third-party service providers
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security reviews

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

I retain your information for:

  • Active clients: Duration of engagement plus 7 years (professional standards)
  • Prospective clients: 2 years from last contact
  • Payment records: 7 years (tax compliance)

You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@jonathancornell.com

Cookies

The Site may use cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Analytics and performance tracking
  • User preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit Site functionality.

Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

I do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe I have inadvertently collected such information, please contact me immediately.

International Visitors

If you are visiting from outside the United States, please note that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

For European Union Visitors (GDPR)

  • Legal basis for processing: Legitimate business interests and contractual necessity
  • Data transfers: Standard contractual clauses
  • Right to lodge complaint: You may file a complaint with your local data protection authority

For California Residents (CCPA)

  • Categories collected: Identifiers, commercial information, internet activity
  • Business purpose: Providing consulting services
  • Your rights: Access, deletion, opt-out of sale (note: I do not sell personal information)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
